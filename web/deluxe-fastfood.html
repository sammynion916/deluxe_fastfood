Our Story
Serving Takoradi Since 1996

For over 25 years, Deluxe Fastfood has been Takoradi's go-to destination for quality fast food. We combine traditional Ghanaian hospitality with modern culinary excellence.

Our mission is simple: serve delicious, affordable meals made with fresh ingredients, prepared with care, and delivered with a smile. Every dish tells our story of passion and dedication.
